Random Thoughts AKA Tweets I Have Tweeted or Would Like to Tweet

I’m a victim of the 140 characters. Okay, sometimes I like to say things that are more than 140 characters but you know what I mean, yeah? Tumblr (not Twitter) has conditioned me to want to share my thoughts every time they pop into my head. Fortunately, I don’t have Tumblr anymore. Unfortunately, I do have a Twitter. Fortunately, I don’t want to use my Twitter1 too much because I have only six followers who probably don’t care about me at all.

However, I do want to release my thoughts to the wild in some sort of fashion instead of keeping it trapped in my brain where it will be lost eventually. So, here they are.
